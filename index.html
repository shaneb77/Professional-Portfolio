About Me

I am a father to two great kids and a husband to one incredibly supportive and hard-working woman. I graduated with a Bachelor of Applied Arts and Sciences from Texas State University in beautiful San Marcos, Texas. I have had a long and successful career in restaurant operations, marketing, sales, and recruiting. For almost 30 years I have given my life to the hospitality industry. As a senior regional director, I was personally responsible for the success of 20 different restaurant locations in Texas and Missouri. For the last five years I have operated a hospitality recruiting firm and resume writing service. This is where I discovered a love for website development and design. With the drastic change to everyday life and the hospitality industry from Covid-19, I decided to invest in myself and my future by entering a certificate program in full stack web development at The University of Texas. I look forward to applying my new skills with my decades of leadership experience!
